What impact has failure had on your life?

  • Failure holds so many people back.
  • Failure prevents so many people from being happy.
  • Failure can be overcome!

  • Michael Hempseed has spoken to everyone from multimillionaires to business owners, to teachers, doctors, lawyers, accountants, to countless teenagers.

  • His message is not just theory, Michael experienced a crushing failure during an audition on Britain's Got Talent.

  • Today Michael travels extensively to tell untold audiences that life does not have to end with failure, that if you fail once you can get back on track.

  • Michael hosts a weekly radio show on New Zealand's PlainsFM 96.9 called Lighthouse of Hope.
