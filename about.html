Michael was born and brought up in Christchurch, New Zealand and is a highly sought after Professional Speaker. He graduated from the University of Canterbury with an Honours Degree in Psychology in the year 2008. He began his career working with challenging and at-risk young people and worked with some of the top 1% of offenders within New Zealand. He has always had a heart for Charities and helping those most in need.

In 2015 Michael started the Employee Solution Service, a specialist HR company that works with unusual or challenging issues within the workplace.

He has spoken to almost every group imaginable, School Children, Prisoners, Social Workers, Multi-millionaires, Doctors, Lawyers, Accountants and many more.

Michael has truly made the most of his life, having visited 34 countries around the world including India, Cambodia, China, Brazil, The Ukraine and many more. In addition to this, he challenged himself to Para-jumping from a perfectly good aeroplane, piloted an aerobatics plane, and enjoys hang-gliding and para-gliding.
