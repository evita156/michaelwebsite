Michael dedicates a lot of time to charitable work. Today Michael mainly provides support to charities though education and training for staff.

Michael's most popular workshop topics include...

Understanding Mental Illness: Michael combines the latest research with practical tools and advice grounded in genuine stories and not just academic knowledge. This workshop is suitable for teachers, counsellors, nurses and social workers.

Preventing Burnout: Helping those in need can be very stressful. Charities often experience high staff turnover. This moving and thought provoking workshop will give your staff practical advice about how to reduce burnout in the workforce. This is suited to almost every industry.

Preventing suicide: If Michael could present one workshop for the rest of his life it would be this one. Many professionals think they know about suicide prevention. After Michael's workshop, staff will have new insights and practical tools to help someone who is suicidal.

Michael uses comedy, magic, moving stories and latest research to provide powerful and inspiring seminars.

For charities Michael offers significantly reduced rates.
